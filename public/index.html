Paint Wars

Paint wars is a spin off of the classic game Bomberman, with a special twist. Players can place bombs and collect power ups similar to the original Bomberman, but the explosion leaves behind the paint splatter. The paint splatter is used to determine a player's score, and players can paint over existing painted tiles to steal points from the opposition. Instead of losing after being struck by a bomb, a player is instead stunned and have their paint and power ups removed. The player with the most points left by the time the clock runs out wins the game.

The game also features a lobby system that allows for simultaneous games to run at once.

Socket Ships

Socket ships is a multiplayer game created using Phaser.io and Socket.io. Players play as a ship that have experience points and health. Players can also attack by moving the mouse and clicking at the direction in which they want to shoot. By hitting opposing players, a ship gains experience points which affect both the speed of the ship and the rate at which it fires. There are also chests that spawn in random locations on the map that players can use to replenish health and gain experience.
